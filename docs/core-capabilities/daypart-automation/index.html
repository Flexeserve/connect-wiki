Daypart Automation

Daypart Automation enables Connect to automatically shift operational behaviour based on the time of day.

This allows teams to align temperature targets and workflows with breakfast, lunch, and evening service periods without constant manual updates.

Benefits​

  • Reduce manual task load for store teams
  • Improve consistency between dayparts
  • Support food quality and compliance goals
  • Help prevent missed transitions during peak periods
