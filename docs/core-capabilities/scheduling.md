# Scheduling

Connect scheduling gives you central control over when units should run, hold, or transition between modes.

By defining schedules once and applying them across your estate, teams can reduce manual intervention, improve consistency, and support food safety compliance during key trading periods.

## Benefits

- Standardise operating windows across all locations
- Reduce energy usage outside active trading periods
- Minimise in-store manual setup and errors
- Support predictable, repeatable service outcomes

Schedules can be created and deployed centrally across all locations, eliminating the need for manual setup in-store. This reduces unnecessary energy usage while ensuring every site operates consistently.

For store teams, this means less manual work. For operators, it means tighter control and improved efficiency.

### Daypart Automation

With built-in daypart automation, Connect automatically adjusts temperatures and configurations throughout the day.

This ensures the right settings are always applied at the right time, supporting consistent food quality and smoother transitions between dayparts.

By removing reliance on manual changes, operators can reduce waste, improve execution, and maintain standards across every location.
